Soft3121

The unofficial BHS-3000/BHS-4000 programming tool

The interface to the left replicates the S3121 alphanumeric programmer, the device used for programming the BHS-3000 and BHS-4000 panels. If you have connected your panel to the RS-485 adapter and you do not see anything on the screen, click the ESC button a few times. (Pressing the Escape key on your keyboard will not work.)

If you don't know the installer access code, click “Download Programming” below to retrieve it.
